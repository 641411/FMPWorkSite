Research & Theories As To The Nature Of Our Universe

The universe we live in is something on a scale that we can scarcely imagine, for as long as we as a species have lived,

we have studied and theorised how exactly it functions, as well as what else it contains that we are unaware of, this website

contains countless years of research on such topics, going over all that we know about the vast expanse of space we inhabit

and theories as to what there could be that we do not yet fully understand or have yet to see,

including research from well known scientists, mathematicians and physicists such as:


    Albert Einstein



    Isaac Newton



    Henri Poincare



    John Dalton



    Murray Gell-Mann



    George Zweig
